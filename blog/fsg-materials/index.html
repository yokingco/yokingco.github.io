Since May 2019, I have served as a Facilitated Study Group (FSG) leader, and more recently a mentor, for Introductory Statistics and Organic Chemistry II courses.

As a facilitator, I was responsible for developing learning materials and facilitating weekly in-person or virtual collaborative and interactive sessions for 20-50 students. As a mentor, I supported new and returning facilitators through weekly check-ins, constructive feedback, performance observations, and coordination of monthly meetings.
