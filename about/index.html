About

Hi! I’m Amanda Yokingco. Welcome to my personal website. I’m so glad you’re here! At the moment, my website is still a work in progress. Nevertheless, I hope you can find what you are looking for. Please check back soon for more updates!

Currently, I’m a fourth-year undergraduate student studying Molecular Biology, Biotechnology, and Applied Statistics at the University of Toronto Scarborough. I’m interested in research at the intersections of biology and data science.

When I’m not studying, I’m also committed to science outreach in my community. I am a site coordinator for [Let’s Talk Science] (https://letstalkscience.ca/) at the University of Toronto Scarborough because I strongly believe in the importance of youth-centered STEM outreach, scientific literacy, as well as equity and inclusion for all students. In my free time, I also enjoy reading, watching movies, scenic walks, and staying caffeinated.
